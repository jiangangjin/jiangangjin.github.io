About me

I am a Professor at School of Naval Architecture, Ocean & Civil Engineering in Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU), Shanghai, China. My research interests lie in the application of large-scale modeling and optimization to transportation and logistics systems, including maritime shipping & port operations, public transportation system optimization. My previous works has been published in international journals, such as Transportation Science, Transportation Research Part A, B, C, E.
